The following is a list of past and future technical activities of this task force:
  • Special session, Information Granulation in Data Science and Scalable Computing,IEEE BIG DATA 2019, December 9-12, 2019, Los Angeles, CA, USA
  • 1st IEEE ICDM Workshop on Autonomous Machine Learning (AML 2019) In conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Data Mining (ICDM 2019), November 8-11, 2019, Beijing, China
  • 2019 IEEE Symposium on Computational Intelligence and Data Mining (IEEE CIDM), part of the 2019 IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence (IEEE SSCI 2019), Xiamen, China, December 6-9, 2019
  • The 7-th Joint International Conferences on Swarm, Evolutionary and Memetic Computing Conference (SEMCCO 2019) & Fuzzy And Neural Computing Conference (FANCCO 2019)
  • Special Issue on “Emerging Computational Intelligence Techniques for Decision Making with Big Data in Uncertain Environments” for IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence, from 2019 to 2020
  • Special Issue on “Fuzzy Rough Sets for Big Data” for IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems from 2018 to 2019
  • Special Issue on “Evolutionary Data Mining for Big Data” for Swarm and Evolutionary Computation from 2018 to 2019
  • Special Issue on “Current Trends of Granular Data Mining for Biomedical Data Analysis” for Information Sciences from 2018 to 2019
